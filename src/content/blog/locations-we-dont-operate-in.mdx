  ## Karen, Kileleshwa, Runda, Kitisuru. Lavington, Muthaiga, Spring Valley, Nyari.

  We don't have properties in any of them.

  Some of these would be on a `/properties?zone=` filter if we did. They're not. The zone chip in the header
  only scrolls to Mlolongo, Syokimau, Baba Dogo, or Thika. If a property in one of the upmarket Nairobi
  suburbs ever reaches us via a family connection or a long-standing client, it goes to the bottom of the
  intake list. It would not be advertised publicly on this site.

  ## Why we don't list anything there

  The math on upmarket residential below 50 million KSH doesn't work for our operator model. The houses
  in those suburbs are sold by their owners, brokered by separate upmarket-only agencies, and the deals
  close privately between buyer and seller before any public listing. By the time the listing would land
  here, the unit is already off the market.

  For apartments in those suburbs, the strata corporations restrict marketing channels. Most buildings
  in Karen / Kitisuru / Runda are strata-titled with owner-elected committees who prefer a single letting
  agent and don't accept walk-in leasing.

  We have tried. Between 2019 and 2022 we had two letting mandates in Kileleshwa and one sale mandate in
  Runda. All three closed through the strata's pre-existing broker. We don't have a permanent mandate in
  any of these suburbs today.

  ## What we do, in four zones

  If you read the [Locations page](/locations), the four zones we operate in are:

  - **Mlolongo** — Mombasa Road, KM 12 to KM 14. Godowns, warehouses, logistics. Operator strongholds.
  - **Syokimau** — Mombasa Road, KM 11. Light industrial, godowns, distributors.
  - **Baba Dogo** — Outering Road. Business parks, commercial blocks, mixed-use.
  - **Thika** — Flame Tree Park Estate. 3-bedroom strata apartments for owner-occupiers.

  The footprint is deliberate. It stays inside one transport corridor and one residential estate. A
  buyer in Karen who wants lakeside acreage, or an operator in Athi River looking for 50,000 sq ft, is
  served better by the broker who sits on that patch of ground year-round than by us.

  ## On the word "yet" in the title

  We put "yet" there because the markets change. Karen in 2024 is not Karen in 2018. Runda in 2030 will
  not be Runda in 2025. We will add zones when the operator math supports a permanent mandate in that
  zone, not before. We will not add a zone for portfolio optics.
