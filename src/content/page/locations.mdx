    body: |
      **District:** Mombasa Road, KM 12 to KM 14
      **Type:** Godowns, warehouses, logistics
      **Typical unit:** 9,000 to 22,500 sq ft
      **Surface:** flat, drained
      **Power:** 200 to 450 kVA at the meter, three-phase
      **Water:** 80 to 180 m³ / day, municipal

      Mlolongo is the operator stronghold of the corridor. Three of our active listings are here.
      The KPLC feeder has documented redundancy on three of the four substations serving KM 11 to
      KM 14, and the Nairobi Water trunk main runs through the eastern plot edge.

      The rail siding at the Inland Container Depot, KM 13, is available to bonded cargo by
      application to KRC. Container dwell time before the 2024 re-survey was 11 days; the 2026
      figure is 6 days.
