  - title: Industrial lease
    body: |
      The standard industrial lease is the same on every listing. There are four terms we publish
      and a small handful that we negotiate per-tenant.

      ## On every listing

      - **Term:** 3-year minimum, renewable by mutual agreement.
      - **Invoicing:** KSH invoiced monthly in arrears, due 5 days after month-end.
      - **Deposit:** 2 months' gross rent, refundable on handover less dilapidations.
      - **Broker fee:** 1 month's gross rent, paid by the tenant at sign-off.

      ## Per-tenant negotiation

      - **Rent-free fit-out period.** Typical: 4 to 8 weeks for a 9,000 sq ft unit, scaled to fit-out
        scope. Most landlords on this corridor do not offer this; we do.
      - **Break clause at year 2.** Available for tenants with a clearly-defined Q4 exit (overseas
        parent relocation, business unit closure). We grant this rarely.
      - **Sub-letting.** Not permitted without written landlord consent. Where granted, it is not
        granted to a direct competitor of the existing tenant in the same corridor.

      ## Not negotiable

      The 3-year term. The deposit amount. The broker fee. The monthly invoicing schedule. Asking
      for changes here is a polite conversation that ends with the same answer; we publish them
      because negotiating them individually takes time off every operator's calendar.

  - title: Residential sale (Flame Tree Park Estate, Thika)
    body: |
      The 3-bedroom strata apartments in Flame Tree Park Estate are sold with title transfer at
      sign-off. There is no lease option on residential.

      ## On every residential listing

      - **Title transfer:** at sign-off. We do not retain a stake in the property after transfer.
      - **Developer warranty:** 6 months on structure and mechanical systems, from sign-off date.
      - **Strata levy:** paid by the buyer from sign-off onwards. The levy schedule is published on
        every listing.
      - **Mortgage finance:** we do not arrange finance. The buyer's bank handles the process; we
        provide the title search and the surveyor's report on request.

      ## Not negotiable

      The 6-month developer warranty. Strata levy amount. Reservations do not transfer between
      buyers; deposits are non-refundable after a 14-day cooling-off window post-sign-off.
